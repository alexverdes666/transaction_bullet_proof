# Bullet Proof — Know before you buy

### The problem, in plain words

Every day, people buy new crypto coins hoping to make money. But many of these coins
are **traps**. They are built so that you *can* buy them… but when you try to **sell**
and take your money out, you can't. Your money is stuck. These traps are called
**"honeypots."**

Other coins are sneakier. They let you sell, but they secretly take a huge cut — you
put in $1,000 and somehow only get back $400. That hidden cut is called a **"tax,"** and
scammers often hide it so you don't notice until it's too late.

By the time you find out, your money is gone. There are no refunds in crypto.

### What this tool does

**Bullet Proof is a safety check you run *before* you spend a single real dollar.**

Think of it like a **crash-test for crypto coins**, or a **test drive in a simulator.**
Instead of risking your own money, it creates a perfect, private copy of the crypto
world — a sandbox — and inside that safe copy it pretends to be a regular shopper:

1. It **buys** the coin with pretend money.
2. It then immediately tries to **sell** it back.
3. It carefully measures what happened to the money at every step.

Because it all happens in a private practice world, **no real money is ever at risk.**

### How you use it (as easy as 1‑2‑3)

You don't need to understand any of the machinery above. Using Bullet Proof is as simple
as checking the weather before you leave the house:

1. **Open the website and create your account.** No software to download, no crypto wallet
   to connect — it runs right in your web browser, on your phone or computer.
2. **Pick a plan and pay securely.** Once you're in, you can start checking coins.
3. **Paste the coin's address.** That's the long code starting with `0x` that you copy from
   wherever the coin is listed (an exchange, a chart site, a friend's message).
4. **Press "Check."** In a few seconds you get your answer.

That's it. You read the result, and you decide — *before* any of your own money is involved.

### What you get back

A clear, simple verdict — like a traffic light:

- 🟢 **SAFE** — You could buy it and sell it back normally. No traps found.
- 🟡 **SUSPICIOUS** — Something looks off (for example, an unusually big hidden fee).
  Be careful.
- 🔴 **HONEYPOT** — Warning! You could buy it, but you would **not** be able to sell it.
  This is a trap. Stay away.

It also tells you the important details in plain numbers, such as *"selling this coin
secretly costs you 90% of your money"* — the kind of thing scammers work hard to hide.

### Why it's trustworthy

- **It uses real-world conditions.** The test runs against a live copy of the actual
  crypto network, so the results reflect how the coin *really* behaves right now — not a
  guess.
- **It actually does the risky thing for you.** Most warnings online are based on reading
  the coin's rulebook. Bullet Proof goes further: it *performs the full buy-and-sell* in
  the safe sandbox and watches the outcome. Actions don't lie.
- **It's built to avoid false alarms.** A lot of care went into making sure a temporary
  internet hiccup never gets mistaken for a real trap, so a red light means a red light.

### Who it's for

- **Everyday buyers** who want a quick gut-check before aping into a new coin.
- **Crypto communities and analysts** who want to screen tokens and protect their members.
- **Anyone** who has ever worried, *"What if I can't get my money back out?"*

### The bottom line

In crypto, the most expensive lesson is learning *after* you've lost the money.
**Bullet Proof lets you learn first — safely, in seconds, for free.**
